01 : About me

My Mission

My interest lies in the advancement in human knowledge through deep space innovation and research. I'm very passionate about space exploration, and hope to one day help mankind become a multi-planetary species. I enjoy being able to visualize and physically see what I am working on and as such have started to lean towards systems engineering to further expand my knowledge in space habitats.

My Story

Since an early age, I have always been fond of puzzles. More specifically crossword, jigsaw, and hidden-object puzzles. As such I developed an analytical way of thinking and developed a talent for math and science which led to my interest in STEM at a young age. Coupled with the fact that it was at this early age that my exposure to science-fiction was at an all time high. Seeing the adventures and different environments on the big screen inspired me to make it into a reality.
